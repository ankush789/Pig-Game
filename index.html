Instructions for the game

  • There are two players playing the game at a time.
  • In every chance, a player can roll a dice until 1 appears on it or player voluntarily presses Hold Button Every time dice is rolled, the score on the dice gets added to the current score of that player (except 1).
  • There are two ways of getting a chance or player gets switched when :
    • 1 appears on the dice.
    • Player voluntarily press Hold Button.
  • On pressing Hold Button, current score of the player gets added to the total score.
  • Player who got 100 points first will be the 🏆 winner of the game.
